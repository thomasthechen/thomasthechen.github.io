Hey friends, thanks for tuning in! I'm starting a blog for a few reasons:

1. It's a place to share interesting ideas I've had myself or learned from others-- whether it be research and courses, or shower-thoughts about my own experiences.
2. I like teaching things that I've studied for a while, like poker. Writing lectures is fun for me (even if no one reads them), and it also improves my understanding of the material. 
3. Writing lectures and (more generally) documenting my thoughts helps me be more concise and intelligible, something that I used to be better at.

If there's a post whose topic resonates with you, but whose content you disagree with, please do not hesitate to message me! I'm super down to discuss as it refines my thoughts too.
